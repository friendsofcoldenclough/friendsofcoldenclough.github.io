Colden Clough is a landscape rich in history and has a multitude of paths meandering amongst old chimneys, temperate rainforest and stunning views. Ancient woodland covers just 2.4% of the UK so we are incredibly lucky to live in an area with such a special place right on our doorstep.

Caring for Colden Clough feels like a great way of giving something back for all that we enjoy and thatâ€™s why the Friends of Colden Clough was set up.

Meeting at 10am on the first Sunday of every month in Eaves Playground, we are a friendly group of people seeking to understand and actively look after the area in a variety of ways. Young or old, everyone is welcome to get involved.

To be kept in the loop with more information, email [friendscoldenclough@gmail.com](mailto:friendscoldenclough@gmail.com) or join our Facebook group ([Friends of Colden Clough](https://www.facebook.com/groups/3797294470486573/)).

We are financially supported by the Rotary Club of Hebden Bridge and Heptonstall Parish Council.
