**Past, Present & Future of the Clough Woodlands**.<br>
Join us for a special guided walk exploring the rich story of the Clough woodlands, where nature and history meet.

<!--more-->

Together we’ll: 

* read the landscape
* discover spring wildflowers and signs of new life
* explore the present day woodland and its wildlife 
* point out the area’s industrial past through hidden remnants/features
* reflect on how the area has changed and what its future could be

Meet at Eaves Playground at 10:00 - suitable for all, just bring sturdy footwear and curiosity! The walk will take two to two and a half hours.

Whether you’re interested in local history, wildlife, or simply enjoy a good walk in nature, we’d love to have you along!
