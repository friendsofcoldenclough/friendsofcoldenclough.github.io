Our June event is a **balsam bash** - clearing invasive Himalayan balsam from the clough.

This year we are concentrating on the upper reaches of the clough to try to stop it spreading down the slopes to the valley bottom, and down the clough using Colden Water.

Meet at [Eaves Playground](https://www.google.com/maps/dir/?api=1&destination=53.745728,-2.027694) at 10:00 - suitable for all, just bring sturdy footwear. The work will take two hours maximum.

## Report from the day

A big thank you to everyone who joined this Himalayan balsam bashing session - we managed to clear a significant area which will make a big difference for our local wildlife and native plants, whilst preventing the spread of seeds down the valley. Great teamwork, well done all! 🌿👏
