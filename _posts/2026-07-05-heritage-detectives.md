**Past, Present & Future of Colden Clough**.<br>
Join us for a special guided walk exploring the rich story of the Clough, where nature and history meet.

<!--more-->

Together we’ll: 

* point out the area’s industrial past through hidden remnants/features
* reflect on how the area has changed and what its future could be

Meet at [Eaves Playground](https://www.google.com/maps/dir/?api=1&destination=53.745728,-2.027694) at 10:00 - suitable for all, just bring sturdy footwear and curiosity! The walk will take two to two and a half hours.

Whether you’re interested in local history, wildlife, or simply enjoy a good walk in nature, we’d love to have you along!
