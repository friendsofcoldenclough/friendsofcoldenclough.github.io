Join us as we come together to plant a living hedge at Eaves Playground, helping us protect, restore, and rewild our shared space. This hedge will act as a natural barrier to discourage bottle digging and prevent soil disturbance, help keep children and visitors safe from the nearby drop-off, and create a thriving habitat for local wildlife.

<!--more-->

We’ll be planting a mixed native tree hedge, combining species to create a stronger, more resilient habitat. Mixed hedgerows provide year-round benefits, offering dense cover for nesting birds, a rich variety of flowers for pollinators like bees, and berries and seeds for wildlife through autumn and winter. Their varied structure also makes them more resistant to pests, disease, and changing weather conditions, while supporting a wider range of species.

Come get involved, learn new skills, and make a real difference in your local environment. Everyone welcome!
